About Us

“Sorry it’s too late”

“It’s detected early. No worries.”

To combat the extreme lack of awareness and stigma surrounding breast cancer, The Breast Cancer Center is an organisation aimed at making each of you more aware about breast cancer, be it risk factors or symptoms or treatment methods or where to get screened. We want to equip you with knowledge in your fight against breast cancer.

Our Mission

  • Helping provide information and raising awareness about breast cancer
  • Conducting awareness drives in partnerships with NGOs and other organisations to raise awareness and decrease stigmatization surrounding breast cancer
  • Conducting sessions with doctors to bring you all the accurate information about breast cancer. Some of these sessions will be live and some recorded

Cancer is a typical symbol of a dangerous disease;
it’s commonly used in day to day life, but I never
thought I’d be a person who’d have to go through it.
I had a bit of chest pain that day and went to the
hospital expecting a tablet or two when they told me I
had a small tumour in my left breast. The pain started from
the initial test in which they took some flesh to determine
the type and stage of cancer. Then they told me all the
precautions I had to take and described in detail the process
to be followed. It involves more pain than one can assume,
both mental and physical. All I want to say is to take
precautions from a young age not to develop this disease.
Don’t smoke, don’t drink too much. I was lucky to get
through this, you may not be.

~ 54 year old woman

Noida, Uttar Pradesh, India
